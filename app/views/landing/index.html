StoryTime

We make raising readers easy.

Long-Run Change

Most reading programs offer parents books and advice— then vanish. StoryTime follows a research-backed model of sticking with parents night after night to help them with the hardest part of the reading habit: keeping it up.

Reading is Fun

StoryTime sends short rhyming stories that segue into activities to get families talking, playing, and sharing, face-to-face. Reading’s fun— for both kids and parents.

Reading is Easy

Parents no longer need to take a public bus to the library, guess at age-appropriate books, and remember to read every night. StoryTime breaks down this complex task into something simple and concrete that, each night, parents can act on.

Simple Technology

Rather than introduce new technology and force parents to learn new habits, StoryTime relies on what over 6 billion people already have and use: mobile texting.

We're here to write a new story.

We built StoryTime to erase the “word gap,” the fact that, by age three, children from poor families have heard 30 million fewer words than those from wealthier families. It sends children’s stories and early-learning activities by text message to parents without books at home. Designed with Yale Child Study experts, it’s an early literacy program for the world’s kids— one that sticks with parents as they sing, play, and share with their kids night after night.

This is a service for parents everywhere.
